About Us and Terms of Services

PixelBones

About us :

Pixelbones wasn't always meant to be an agency. It began as a game development studio — a passion project between friends who wanted to build worlds together. But as artists, we saw a bigger opportunity: a space where we could do everything. Design, tech, collaborative, and still build games when the time is right. That vision evolved into Pixelbones, officially launched in August 2025, and built to empower artists, serve clients with meaning, and keep creativity at the center of it all.


Pixelbones is a digital agency/ Multidisciplinary Creative Hub driven by bold ideas and creative design. Founded and Owned by Waale Adnan Midha, a visual artist and creative director with a passion for expressive storytelling, and co-founded by The Randomizer, the studio was built to challenge convention and craft visuals that leave a lasting impact.


Based in Bangladesh, Pixelbones works with clients across the globe through trusted creative marketplaces. While our reach is international, our roots are local — and we're proud to begin by welcoming artists and students from Bangladesh into our growing creative community. This is just the starting point of a studio built to connect, empower, and create without borders.


Our team is made up of skilled artists, designers, and creative thinkers — each chosen for their unique strengths and creative edge. Together, we deliver high-quality design from just ideas in 2D and 3D, tailored to both business needs and individual visions.


Beyond client work, Pixelbones is home to Pixelbones Academy, where our lead artists mentor aspiring creatives. We believe in sharing knowledge, empowering others, and keeping the spirit of art alive through education and collaboration.


Pixelbones was created to centralize design services and creative education — a space where artistry is valued and careers are shaped with intention. We exist to help artists grow, not as employees or freelancers, but as creators with identity, voice, and vision. Here, artists are matched to projects that respect their craft, and clients receive more than just design — they get visuals that speak meaning, built on understanding and purpose.


At Pixelbones, we handle every client's project with care. We take time to listen — to your story, your motive, your aim, and your imagination. Understanding your vision is the first step in expressing it with clarity and meaning. We don't just deliver visuals; we design designs that reflect your intent, shaped through thoughtful collaboration and artistic precision.


We also hold a strict creative boundary that's part of our Terms of Service: Pixelbones does not offer portrait services depicting living beings with complete facial features — and we don't encourage it. This rule reflects our values around artistic integrity, privacy, and respectful collaboration. We appreciate clients who honor these boundaries and share our commitment to meaningful, intentional design.



Whether you're an artist looking to grow, a client with a vision to bring to life, or a student ready to learn — you're welcome here. Pixelbones is more than a studio; it's a space for creativity, collaboration, and careers in motion. Step in, speak your truth, and let's build something meaningful together.
